Climate Data Science & Modeling

This tutorial is a guide to understanding and utilizing climate data through advanced computational methods, whether you're an experienced scientist seeking to enhance your data analysis prowess with Python's capabilities or a curious student eager to learn about climate science. We're here to help you grasp the complexities of climate change and contribute to a sustainable future using data-driven approaches. Everyone is welcome to join this inclusive community of learners and contributors, regardless of your background or level of expertise. Let's start this exciting learning journey together and grow in our scientific explorations. This project was led by Dr. Fahad.
