Python Tutorial for Climate Science

Discover the exciting world of climate science with this Python tutorial designed for aspiring researchers and enthusiasts. No prior programming experience needed! Gain valuable insights and hands-on skills to analyze, visualize, and model climate data effectively. Explore diverse techniques like seasonal analysis, statistical analysis, and GIS applications for remote sensing. Dig into flood mapping with Google Earth Engine and discover NASA's ECCO/MITgcm data. While Python plays a role, this holistic approach fosters a deeper understanding of climate science beyond programming. Gain confidence in interpreting climate data and conducting impactful research.
